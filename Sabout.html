About Our College

Welcome to Rungta College of Excellence – a pioneer in higher education, research, and innovation. Founded in the year 2000, the college has continually evolved into one of the most prestigious institutions in the region.

Our institution offers a wide variety of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Management, Computer Applications, and Commerce. With a dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art infrastructure, we provide a dynamic learning environment to shape the future leaders of tomorrow.

We believe in fostering a culture of excellence, discipline, and innovation while also encouraging holistic development in academics, sports, arts, and community engagement.

Our Mission

To provide a transformative education that nurtures creativity, critical thinking, and ethical values, preparing students to thrive in a rapidly evolving global society.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a center of academic excellence, research, and innovation, shaping the future through knowledge and empowerment.

Our Core Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Inclusiveness, and Social Responsibility.

Key Achievements

  • Accredited with NAAC ‘A+’ Grade
  • Over 300+ campus placements every year
  • ISO 9001:2015 Certified Institution
  • Highly qualified faculty with industry and research expertise
  • Modern labs, smart classrooms, and innovation hubs
  • National-level sports and cultural achievements
